Our project is about violence: It's impact on the world, It's causes, and it's multiple variations. Violence is terrible and should not be condoned by anyone. This project is for educational purposes only.

There are many causes for violence the most prominent are racism and homophobia because these ideas are so widespread and target large groups.

Violence can have many effects on society though they are mostly negative. Some examples are: death, poor economic health, and a lack of resources. However, not all of the problems are physical. Violence can lead to depression and the suicidal thoughts which can lead to further violence.

Violence comes in many forms all of which are equally terrible. Most know of physical violence like murders, shooting and more. However, violence can also occur in one's mind due to mental abuse.
